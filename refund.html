REFUND POLICY

1. Introduction

   At Gym Management System, we strive to provide exceptional services to our members. We understand that circumstances may arise where you need to cancel your membership or request a refund. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are granted.

2. Membership Cancellation

  Monthly Memberships: Members may cancel their monthly membership by providing a 30-day written notice. Cancellation requests should be submitted in person at the gym reception or via email to contact@gymManagement.com

  Annual Memberships: Annual memberships require a commitment for the full term. Early termination may be subject to an early cancellation fee, unless otherwise specified in your membership agreement.

3. Refund Eligibility

 Refunds are considered under the following circumstances:

  Medical Reasons: If a member is unable to continue their membership due to a medical condition, a prorated refund may be issued upon receipt of a valid medical certificate.

  Relocation: Members relocating more than 15 kilometers/miles away from any of our locations may be eligible for a prorated refund upon providing proof of new residence.

  Service Dissatisfaction: If a member is dissatisfied with our services, we encourage them to discuss their concerns with management. Refunds for dissatisfaction are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

4. Non-Refundable Fees

 The following fees are non-refundable:

  Enrollment Fees: Initial enrollment or setup fees are non-refundable.

  Late Cancellation Fees: Fees incurred from late cancellations of classes or personal training sessions are non-refundable.

5. Refund Process

 To request a refund:

1.  Submit a written request detailing the reason for cancellation and refund to contact@gymManagement.com.

2.  Attach any supporting documentation, such as medical certificates or proof of relocation.

3.  Our team will review your request and respond within 7 business days.

   Approved refunds will be processed using the original payment method within 15 days of approval.

6. Changes to Refund Policy

   Gym Management System reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Members will be notified of significant changes through email or a notice on our website.
