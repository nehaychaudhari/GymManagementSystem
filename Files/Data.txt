Weight Maintenance Balanced Diet Plan  
(For Indian Vegetarian & Non-Vegetarian Diets)  

Balanced Diet Essentials:  
- Carbs (50-60%): Roti, rice, oats, millets (jowar, bajra, ragi)  
- Proteins (20-30%): Dal, paneer, eggs, chicken, fish  
- Healthy Fats (10-15%): Ghee, nuts, seeds, olive oil  
- Fiber & Micronutrients: Fruits, vegetables, sprouts  

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 1. Vegetarian Diet Plan  

Early Morning (6:30 - 7:00 AM)  
- 1 glass lukewarm water (lemon & honey optional)  
- 5-6 almonds (soaked) + 2 walnuts  
- 1 cup green tea / herbal tea  

Breakfast (8:00 - 9:00 AM)  
- Option 1: Poha/upma with peanuts & vegetables + buttermilk  
- Option 2: Multigrain paratha (paneer/methi/aloo) + curd  
- Option 3: Oats porridge with fruits & seeds  
- 1 glass lukewarm water after breakfast  

Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM - 12:00 PM)  
- 1 fruit (banana/apple/orange/papaya)  
- 5-6 soaked mishri or dates  

Lunch (1:00 - 2:00 PM)  
- 1-2 chapati / brown rice  
- 1 bowl dal / rajma / chole / sprouts  
- 1 bowl vegetable sabzi  
- 1 bowl curd / buttermilk  
- Salad (cucumber, carrot, tomato)  

Evening Snack (4:30 - 5:00 PM)  
- 1 cup green tea / black tea (without sugar)  
- Roasted makhana / peanuts / sprouts chaat  

Dinner (7:30 - 8:30 PM)  
- 1-2 chapati / khichdi / millets dosa  
- 1 bowl vegetable curry / dal  
- 1 bowl soup (tomato / spinach / mushroom)  

Before Bed (10:00 - 10:30 PM)  
- 1 glass warm milk with turmeric  
- 1 small piece jaggery  

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 2. Non-Vegetarian Diet Plan  

Early Morning (6:30 - 7:00 AM)  
- 1 glass lukewarm water (lemon & honey optional)  
- 5-6 almonds (soaked) + 2 walnuts  
- 1 cup green tea / black coffee  

Breakfast (8:00 - 9:00 AM)  
- Option 1: 2 boiled eggs + brown bread + milk  
- Option 2: Scrambled eggs with vegetables + multigrain toast  
- Option 3: Chicken sandwich + fresh juice  

Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM - 12:00 PM)  
- 1 fruit (banana/apple/orange)  
- 5-6 soaked mishri or dates  

Lunch (1:00 - 2:00 PM)  
- 1-2 chapati / brown rice  
- 1 bowl grilled chicken / fish curry / egg curry  
- 1 bowl dal / vegetable sabzi  
- 1 bowl curd / buttermilk  
- Salad (cucumber, carrot, tomato)  

Evening Snack (4:30 - 5:00 PM)  
- 1 cup black coffee / green tea  
- Roasted chicken / boiled eggs  

Dinner (7:30 - 8:30 PM)  
- 1 bowl grilled fish / chicken soup  
- 1-2 chapati / millets dosa  
- 1 bowl vegetable sabzi / dal  

Before Bed (10:00 - 10:30 PM)  
- 1 glass warm milk with turmeric  
- 1 small piece jaggery
